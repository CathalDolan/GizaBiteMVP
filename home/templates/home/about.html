About GizaBite

Gizabite helps you easily identify allergens in your recipes and count the calories in individual dishes. To identify allergens you simply need to select your ingredients from the suggestions list provided. Counting calories is just as quick and easy, as you need only add the quantities to your ingredients.

Calorific calculations and the identification of food allergens is done using data form numerous sources including datasets from the USDA, McCance_Widdowsons CoFID 2021, ANSES-CIQUAL 2020 and manufacturer information provided either on their own websites or those of their distributors.

Disclaimer: Owing to the nature of fresh foods and the prepartion of cooked foods, it is impossible to provide an accurate calculation for the number of calories in a dish. The results on this platform are provided as a guide only and allow for realistic comparrisons by consumers to identify high and low calorie dishes.

Allergen identification is based upon known allergens in raw foods, plus data provided by manufacturers and distributors. To this end we are reliant upon the information provided by those thrid parties.
